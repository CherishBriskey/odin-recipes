Miso Soup Recipe

Miso Soup is a recipe is something any grandma or restuarant can make!

It is very sastifying and is something that you would want to have almost everyday.

Making Miso Soup can be made anytime and during any weather conditions.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi granules
  • Miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

How to Make Miso Soup Step-By-Step

  1. Bring the water and dashi granules to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in the tofu and green onions.
  4. Simmer and serve.
