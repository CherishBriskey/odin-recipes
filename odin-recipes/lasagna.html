Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a recipe is something any grandma or restuarant can make

It is very sastifying and is something that you would want to have almost everyday.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but you will enjoy making this with your family.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic:
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • noodles
  • Eggs
  • Cheeses

How to Make the Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
